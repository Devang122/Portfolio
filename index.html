About Me

Full-Stack Researcher & Mental Health Advocate

I'm Dr. Ajit Kumar Singh, Assistant Professor at AIBAS, Amity University Rajasthan, specializing in Psychology and Wellbeing Research.

With expertise spanning Positive Psychology, Cognitive Psychology, and Research Methodology, I dedicate myself to creating meaningful psychological insights that improve people's lives.

My research focuses on building resilience, understanding mental processes, and developing therapeutic interventions that make a real difference in the field.
